                                       SAMTools.jl
                            Created by: Nathanael Zhixin Wong

SAMTools is a Julia package specifically designed to automate post-processing and analysis of SAM (System of Atmospheric Modeling) Output.

If you wish to contribute to SAMTools.jl, please contact me at:
- nathanaelwong@fas.harvard.edu
- or you can also submit a pull-request if you've already created something!
